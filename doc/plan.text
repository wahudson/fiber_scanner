## Concept Plan 2019-10-30
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Objective:
    Build a 1-dimension voice coil fiber scanner proof of concept.

Products:
    v1 - Paper design concepts.

    v2 - Photo diode on optical fiber.  Explore illumination requirement.

    v3 - Move fiber by hand.

    v4 - add lens on slotted rail

    v5 - add voice coil movement.

    v6 - add power op-amp to drive voice coil.

    v7 - add digitizer to photodiode.

System Concept:

    Fiber end is mounted on voice coil.
    Fiber pigtail has ST connector.
    Photodiode has ST connector.
    TIA signal conditioner for photodiode.
    T-slot rail for holding optical components.
    Use a 10X loup lens to focus image on fiber tip (on voice coil).
    Pendulum to hold object target.  Provides Y-displacement.
    Digitizer to read photodiode.
    DAC and power amplifier drive voice coil.
    Raspberry Pi provides control for DAC and digitizer.
